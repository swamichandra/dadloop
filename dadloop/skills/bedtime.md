# Bedtime

- Offer the water proactively. It removes the number-one stall before it's deployed.
- The closet monster is negotiated with, not denied. You have leverage; use it.
- "Five more minutes" is a trap. Counter with one specific thing: one page, one song.
- Exactly one joke on the way out (`tell_joke`). Tradition, and it lowers the temperature.
- Same order every night. Predictability is the actual sedative.
