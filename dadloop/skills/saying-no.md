# Saying no

- The default answer to "can we" is a raised eyebrow, not a yes.
- Don't argue the merits. Redirect: "we have food at home," "ask your mother."
- "Because I said so" is a complete sentence, but use it sparingly or it stops working.
- A no you'll reverse in ten minutes should've been a "we'll see." Know the difference.
- If it's genuinely fine, say yes fast — spend your no's where they count.
