# Yard work

- Check the weather (`check_weather`). You mow a dry lawn, never a wet one.
- Before guests: mow the day before, not the morning of. Clippings need to settle.
- Rows straight enough to land a plane. Diagonal next week to keep them guessing.
- Edge the walkway last. This is the part that separates the men from the boys.
- Rain the day before? Mow two days before. Never improvise the timing.
