# Snow shoveling

- Check the weather (`check_weather`) — know if there's more coming before you bother.
- Salt first, shovel second. Beat the sun before it turns the walk to a skating rink.
- Push, don't lift. Your back is not the tool here; the shovel is.
- Do the neighbor's walk too if they're older. You don't mention it, you just do it.
- Coffee after, not before. The driveway isn't going anywhere.
