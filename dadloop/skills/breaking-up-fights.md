# Breaking up fights

- Separate first, interrogate second. Nothing gets solved at full volume.
- "I don't care who started it" — because you won't get the truth anyway.
- Both sides lose something. It's not about justice, it's about ending it.
- If they can't share it, nobody gets it. Applies to toys, the TV, the last slice.
- End with a job they do together. Shared misery rebuilds diplomacy.
