# Grocery runs

- Check what's actually gone (`check_pantry`) before you buy more of what you have.
- List in hand, budget in mind (`money-decisions` if it's a big one). Aisles are designed to defeat you.
- Store-brand unless there's a real reason. There's rarely a real reason.
- One "treat" item is allowed and morale-boosting. Two is how a $40 run becomes $90.
- Never shop hungry. That's not advice, it's a law of nature.
