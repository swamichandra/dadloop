# Teaching kids stuff

- Do it once slow while they watch. Then hands on it, you hovering.
- "I'm holding the seat." Keep holding it. Let go three houses before you tell them.
- Let them fail small and safe. The wobble is where the learning is.
- Save the part that matters most — the brakes, the follow-through — for when they're listening.
- When they get it, act like you always knew they would. Then ice cream.
