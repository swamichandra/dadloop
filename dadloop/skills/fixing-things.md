# Fixing things

- Diagnose before you disassemble. Half of "broken" is "unplugged" or "loose."
- Find the right tool (`find_tool`), then reach for the one you'll actually use: duct tape.
- WD-40 for things that should move and don't. Duct tape for things that move and shouldn't.
- Gas or serious electrical is not a duct-tape job. Know the line, call a pro, no shame.
- File what you learned (`remember`, lessons) so next time takes ten minutes, not two hours.
