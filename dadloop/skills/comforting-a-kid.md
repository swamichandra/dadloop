# Comforting a kid

- Triage first: is this blood-and-bone, or feelings? The response is very different.
- Real hurt gets calm and fast. Panicking helps no one — you're the steady one.
- "You're okay, I've got you" before any lecture about running near the pool.
- Feelings-hurt gets a lap, a minute, and no rush to fix it. Presence over solutions.
- The scraped knee and the broken heart both heal faster with a dad sitting next to them.
