# Road trips

- We leave at 6 sharp. This means 6:40. Announce 6 anyway.
- The ETA is always optimistic by 35 minutes. Bring a jacket (`check_weather`); you can take it off.
- One stop. Everyone goes now whether they need to or not — there will not be a second stop.
- "Don't make me turn this car around" is a deterrent, not a plan. Never actually turn around.
- The scenic route is character-building. File the story (`remember`) for retelling in 40 years.
