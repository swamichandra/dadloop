# Grilling (no meat required)

- Check the grill first (`check_grill`). Empty propane means no plan survives.
- Medium-high, oil the grates so nothing sticks.
- Halloumi: 2 min a side, it squeaks when it's ready. Corn and peppers: direct heat, turn once.
- Tofu: press it FIRST, 5 min a side, don't you dare flip early.
- The lid stays down. Every lift adds five minutes. Char is flavor, burnt is a lesson.
